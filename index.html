Board of Technical & Engineering Professionals

Board of Technical & Engineering Professionals is a Professional organization, constituted by the Government of the Punjab, Manpower and Overseas Pakistanis. In addition to its accreditation with various top ranked organizations, colleges / universities abroad to excel distance learning on fast track, online, competency based and regular education for life experienced, matured and on-job professionals. BTEP operates on Public-Private Partnership. Active Participation of Employers Federation of Pakistan [EFP] and Private Sector Businessmen have created the success story of the Board of Technical & Engineering Professionals, Rawalpindi. It is affiliated with Foreign Office, Foreign universities & colleges and is engaged in Distance Learning Education. BTEP is engaged to identify, develop and arrange Vocational, Technical / Professional and IT Training Programs. Training Programs are flexible, demand driven and cost effective with maximum participation from the Employers. BTEP, Rawalpindi also offers tailor-made courses, according to the customers needs. It conducts Training Needs Assessment Surveys and schedules its training programs accordingly.
